## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[13] Klatt (1980)** - The foundational cascade/parallel formant synthesizer specification, directly applicable to understanding how formant parameters drive Klatt synthesis

- **[6] Flanagan (1965)** and **[7] Fant (1970)** - Classic formant bandwidth and acoustic theory references providing empirical validation data

- **[11] Klatt & Klatt (1990)** - Voice quality variations analysis using formants, essential for understanding how different glottal phases affect synthesis

- **[16] Markel & Gray (1976)** - Linear prediction fundamentals underlying the covariance method used throughout this paper

- **[23] Fant, Liljencrants & Lin (1985)** - The LF glottal model that parametrically describes glottal flow, directly relevant to understanding glottal closure dynamics
