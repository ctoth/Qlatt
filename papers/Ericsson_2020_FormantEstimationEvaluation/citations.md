## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Monsen & Engebretson (1983)** - Foundational comparison of manual formant tracking vs. automatic LPC estimation; provides historical baseline for evaluating formant estimation accuracy.

2. **Escudero et al. (2009)** - Introduces the optimized formant ceiling procedure being evaluated; critical for understanding the optimization strategy based on variance minimization.

3. **Watanabe (2001)** - Describes inverse-filter control (IFC), an automated method that handles high fundamental frequency better than LPC-based approaches; important alternative to explore.

4. **Dissen et al. (2019)** - Deep learning approach to formant estimation that adapts to speaker characteristics; represents recent progress on the fundamental frequency problem.

5. **Fant (1960, 1970)** - Foundational acoustic theory of speech production; provides theoretical framework for understanding formants and vocal tract resonances.
