## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Doval et al. (2006)** - "The spectrum of glottal flow models" - Unifies Rosenberg, KLGLOTT88, LF, and R++ models under common 5-parameter framework, essential for understanding relationships between different glottal models.

2. **Fant et al. (1985)** - "A four-parameter model of glottal flow" - Original LF model definition paper, foundational for all subsequent work on parametric glottal source modeling.

3. **Feugère et al. (2017)** - "Cantor Digitalis: Chironomic parametric synthesis of singing" - Implements LFLM model in real-time musical context, demonstrates practical application of the linear-filter glottal models.

4. **Fant (1995)** - "The LF-model revisited: Transformations and frequency domain analysis" - Derives the Rd parameter transformation equations that unify time-domain and spectral interpretations of the LF model.

5. **Childers & Lee (1991)** - "Vocal quality factors: Analysis, synthesis and perception" - Establishes perceptual framework for voice quality parameters and third-order filter representation of glottal models.
