- **[6] Childers & Lee 1991 - Vocal Quality Factors**: Seminal work on voice quality parameter extraction; relevant for understanding the relationship between source and voice quality (already in papers/)

- **[10] Kain & Macon 1998 - Spectral Voice Conversion**: The voice conversion application framework that motivates this paper's glottal source modeling approach
