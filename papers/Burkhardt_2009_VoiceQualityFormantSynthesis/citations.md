## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **[7] Klatt & Klatt 1990** - The foundational KLSYN88 paper describing the revised glottal source model with OQ, TL, DI, FL parameters. Essential for understanding the parameter space being manipulated.

2. **[5] Gobl & Ni Chasaide 2003** - Comprehensive overview of voice quality and emotion connection. Used KLSYN88 to simulate phonation types and related them to emotional impression via perception experiments.

3. **[4] Laver 1980** - The canonical reference for phonation type terminology (modal, breathy, whispery, creaky, tense, lax). Defines the voice quality categories being simulated.

4. **[8] Fant, Liljencrants & Lin 1985** - The LF (Liljencrants-Fant) four-parameter glottal flow model. Foundation for understanding Ee, te, tp, Ta parameters and their acoustic correlates.

5. **[14] Klatt 1990 (KLATTALK)** - Unpublished but cited for tracheal coupling pole/zero frequencies used in breathy voice simulation (FNP/FNZ at 550 Hz, FTP/FTZ at 2100 Hz).
