## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Klatt & Klatt 1990** [1] - Foundational work on voice quality analysis and synthesis, directly applies LF model parameters to male/female voice characterization
2. **Veldhuis 1998** [2] - Precursor to this perceptual study, examining spectral consequences of glottal parameter variations
3. **Moore et al. 1997** [4] - The auditory model (5-stage loudness prediction model) that provides the theoretical basis for excitation pattern computation in this work
4. **Childers & Lee 1991** [7] - Source of empirical LF R-parameter sets from diverse voice qualities used in the perceptual analysis
5. **Karlsson & Liljencrants 1996** [8] - Provides the diverse voice quality parameter sets that demonstrate the 1D trajectory property in perceptual space
