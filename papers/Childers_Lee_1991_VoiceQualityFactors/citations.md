## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Fant et al. (1985)** - The original LF glottal model paper, foundational for understanding glottal source modeling referenced throughout

2. **Klatt (1980, 1987)** - Complete specification of the Klatt cascade/parallel formant synthesizer and comprehensive TTS review, essential for formant synthesis context

3. **Flanagan (1972)** - Fundamental speech analysis and synthesis theory establishing source-filter model principles

4. **Holmes (1973)** - Original work on glottal waveform influence on parallel formant synthesizer naturalness

5. **Isshiki et al. (1978)** - Turbulent noise characteristics in dysphonia, directly cited for breathiness modeling approach
