## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **[2] Fant, Liljencrants, Lin (1985)** - The original LF model paper; foundational for glottal flow modeling in speech synthesis.

2. **[4] Klatt & Klatt (1990)** - Key paper on voice quality analysis using the KLGLOTT88 model, directly cited in Qlatt project.

3. **[7] Hanson (1995)** - Female glottal characteristics and acoustic correlates; essential for gender-based voice synthesis.

4. **[15] Fant (1995)** - LF model revisited with transformations and frequency domain analysis; theoretical foundation for spectral methods.

5. **[34] Hanson (1997)** - Glottal characteristics and acoustic correlates including H1-H2 relationship to open quotient; widely cited in prosody/voice quality work.
