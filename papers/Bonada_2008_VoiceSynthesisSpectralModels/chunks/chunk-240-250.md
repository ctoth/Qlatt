## Implementation Notes

### Voice Transformation Parameters
The systematic testing with T_pitch and T_timbre reveals the parameter space used for voice transformation:
- Pitch can be shifted down to 70% (T_pitch=0.7) or up to 125% (T_pitch=1.25) of original
- Timbre independently adjusted +/- 5% around neutral
- Phase model can be optionally applied for additional quality

### Note Attack Templates
Six distinct attack templates for singing synthesis - could inform amplitude envelope shaping:
1. Normal - standard attack
2. Smooth - gradual onset
3. Smooth long - extended gradual onset
4. Strong accent - sharp attack
5. Low exaggerated - emphasized low-frequency onset
6. Sexy - presumably breathy/aspirated attack

These template names suggest perceptually-motivated envelope categories that could enhance TTS expressiveness.
