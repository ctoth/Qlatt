## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Klatt & Klatt (1990)** - Comprehensive reference on KLSYN88 synthesizer and voice quality variations; central to understanding the complete parameter system being abstracted here.

2. **Fant (1960)** - Foundational acoustic theory of speech production; provides the theoretical basis for vocal tract modeling and formant calculations.

3. **Stevens (1971, 1987)** - Stevens' own work on aerodynamic noise generation and acoustic-articulatory relationships; critical for understanding the physics-based mappings proposed in this paper.

4. **Shadle (1985)** - Detailed analysis of fricative acoustics and noise generation; essential for the frication source amplitude calculations in the HL-KL mapping.

5. **Fant, Liljencrants & Lin (1985)** - Four-parameter glottal flow model; provides the modern glottal source model that enables simplified parametric control in the HL system.
