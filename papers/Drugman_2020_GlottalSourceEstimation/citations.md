## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Fant et al. (1985)** - "A four-parameter model of glottal flow" - The foundational LF model specification used in all synthetic speech tests

2. **Alku et al. (2002)** - "Normalized amplitude quotient for parametrization of the glottal flow" - Defines NAQ, the most discriminative voice quality feature

3. **Doval & d'Alessandro (2006)** - "The spectrum of glottal flow models" - Comprehensive spectral analysis of glottal models including relationship between time and frequency domain parameters

4. **Childers & Lee (1991)** - "Vocal quality factors: Analysis, synthesis, and perception" - Classic paper on voice quality synthesis we already have

5. **Walker & Murphy (2007)** - "A review of glottal waveform analysis" - Comprehensive review of glottal analysis methods, good for understanding the broader landscape
