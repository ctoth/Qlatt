## Key Citations for Follow-up

- **[1] Fant et al. 1985** - The foundational four-parameter LF model definition; essential reference for understanding the parametric glottal source model
- **[12] Kawahara et al. 2015** - Direct competitor approach to aliasing-free glottal source implementation using anti-aliasing functions; important for understanding alternative solutions
- **[17] Fant 1995** - Frequency domain analysis of the LF model with transformations; directly relevant background work that this paper extends
- **[36] Doval et al. 2003** - Voice source as causal/anticausal linear filter; theoretical foundation for Gobl's frequency-domain approach
- **[20] Gobl 2003** - Gobl's own PhD thesis on voice source production and perception; foundational for understanding the author's broader perspective on glottal modeling
