## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Fant et al. 1985 [11]** - Original LF-model paper. Essential foundation for understanding the four-parameter glottal flow model.

2. **Fant 1995 [12]** - LF-model revisited. Contains the prediction equations for deriving Ra, Rg, Rk from Rd. Critical for implementation.

3. **Klatt & Klatt 1990 [16]** - Already in papers/. Voice quality variations analysis - provides context for AH, TL parameters in Klatt synthesizer.

4. **Hermes 1991 [14]** - Perception of breathy vowels. Explains why pitch-synchronous noise integrates perceptually while stationary noise segregates.

5. **Strik 1998 [24]** - Automatic LF parameterization. Useful for analysis/copy-synthesis applications and validating LF implementations.
