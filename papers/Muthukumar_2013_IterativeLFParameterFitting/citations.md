## Key Citations for Follow-up

1. **Fant, Liljencrants & Lin 1985 [17]** - Original LF model paper. Foundational reference, already in project as `Fant_1985_LFModelGlottalFlow`.

2. **Fant & Lin 1988 [23]** - "Frequency domain interpretation and derivation of glottal flow parameters" - Defines OQ, SQ, RQ quotient parameters. Important for understanding F0-independent parameterization.

3. **Raitio et al. 2011 [2]** - Comprehensive IAIF + HMM synthesis paper. Details the inverse filtering method used here and its integration with statistical synthesis.

4. **Kane & Gobl 2009 [8]** - Automatic LF parameterization combining time and frequency domain. Alternative fitting approach that might be more efficient than gradient descent.

5. **Gobl & Chasaide 2003 [5]** - Amplitude-based source parameters for voice quality. Relevant for understanding how LF parameters relate to perceptual voice quality dimensions.
